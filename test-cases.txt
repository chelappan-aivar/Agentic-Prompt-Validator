  marketing    80-250     Marketing / Copywriting
  technical   100-400     Technical / Engineering

ROUTING LOGIC:
  APPROVE  -> composite >= 0.85 AND severity == LOW AND confidence >= 0.7
  REVIEW   -> composite < 0.5  OR  severity == HIGH  OR  confidence < 0.7
  REFINE   -> otherwise (auto-loop, capped at 3 iterations)

COMPOSITE SCORE: 0.25 * token + 0.35 * clarity + 0.40 * safety
